Alma Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2026

Alma is a voice journaling app that uses AI to help you reflect on your thoughts. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data Alma collects, how it's used, and your rights.

What Data Alma Collects

  • Voice recordings: When you start a journal session, Alma records your voice through your device microphone. Recordings are used for transcription and are not stored permanently — they are discarded after being converted to text.
  • Conversation transcripts: The text of your journal sessions (both your words and AI responses) is stored locally on your device.
  • Personal knowledge: Facts and themes that Alma learns about you over time (e.g., names of people you mention, topics you care about) are stored locally on your device.
  • Session summaries and patterns: Structured summaries, detected patterns, and insights are generated from your sessions and stored locally on your device.

Where Your Data Is Stored

All journal data (transcripts, personal knowledge, patterns, summaries) is stored locally on your device in an SQLite database. Alma does not operate a cloud backend and does not upload your journal data to any server controlled by Alma.

Third-Party AI Services

To provide speech-to-text, text-to-speech, and AI conversation features, Alma sends data to the following third-party API providers during active use:

  • Google Gemini (Google LLC) — used for voice transcription, text-to-speech, AI conversation, and text embeddings
  • xAI (Grok) (xAI Corp) — optionally used for AI conversation if configured
  • OpenAI (OpenAI, Inc.) — optionally used for AI conversation if configured

When you speak during a session, your audio and/or transcript text is sent to these services for processing. These services process the data according to their own privacy policies. Alma uses API-level access, meaning your data is generally not used to train these providers' models, but you should review their policies directly:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • xAI Privacy Policy

What Alma Does NOT Do

  • Alma does not sell your data to anyone
  • Alma does not share your data with advertisers
  • Alma does not collect analytics or usage tracking
  • Alma does not require an account or login
  • Alma does not store your data on any cloud server

Your API Keys

Alma requires you to provide your own API keys for the AI services it uses. These keys are stored locally on your device and are sent directly to the respective API providers. Alma does not have access to your API keys beyond your device.

Deleting Your Data

You can delete all your data at any time from within the app:

Settings → Preferences → Delete All Data

This permanently removes all sessions, transcripts, personal knowledge, patterns, theories, and insights from your device. This action cannot be undone.

Children's Privacy

Alma is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the effective date at the top of this page.
